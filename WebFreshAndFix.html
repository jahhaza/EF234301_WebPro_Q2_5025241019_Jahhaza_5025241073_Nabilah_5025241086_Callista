FreshAndFix is a modern digital grocery platform designed to help families, households, and small businesses access high-quality groceries at stable, transparent prices. Our mission is to make shopping for essential items easier, safer, and more affordable — all through a clean, intuitive, and hassle-free online experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need an account to shop?

To protect your orders, save your addresses, and maintain accurate order history and invoices.

How do I qualify for wholesale discounts?

Each product has different quantity thresholds. Wholesale pricing is applied instantly when you reach the minimum required quantity.

Are the products original and safe?

Yes — FreshAndFix only partners with certified suppliers and verified distributors.

How long does shipping take?

Delivery time varies by courier and region. Estimated delivery times are shown during checkout.

Can I change my address after placing an order?

Address changes may be possible before the order is processed. Contact support immediately.

Do you accept COD?

Availability depends on courier partnerships and current promotions.

Can I schedule deliveries?

Yes — certain couriers support scheduled delivery windows.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Please contact us within 48 hours with photos. We will assist you with replacement or refund.
